
#  Influence Maximization resources

Surveys

●	[LI, Yuchen; FAN, Ju; WANG, Yanhao; and TAN, Kian-Lee. Influence maximization on social graphs: A survey. (2018). IEEE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering. 30, (10), 1852-1872.
